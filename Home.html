Robin Shakedown

Georgia-born, Atlanta-raised, all American. The upbeat glam rock sounds found in Robin's music are inspired by Tom Petty, Patti Smith, and of course Stewart Copeland's delightful Spyro the Dragon soundtrack. 

Robin (she/her) exists at the intersection of many identities - musician, songwriter, transgender woman, LGBTQ+ activist, and certified rock n' roll badass.
